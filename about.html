Welcome to my Journey!

My name is Spencer Rose. I'm from Clearwater, Florida and a current student-athlete at Tufts University. At Tufts, I'm double-majoring in Computer Science (BS) and Economics (BA) and will graduate this upcoming Spring. My interests include hockey and finance, both of which I am deeply passionate about.

One goal of my website is to demonstrate how economics and computer science are integral to shaping the future. Furthermore, I hope to share my experiences and insights, inspiring others to learn from my journey and athletic endeavors. Enjoy!
